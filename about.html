About Us

The Bike Shop is an Irish-owned company that prides itself on excellent customer service and a unique approach to sourcing and selling bikes through Ireland & Europe. We were established in 2014 as a simple warehouse project and have since grown to stock a wide range of international brands whilst maintain a Local Bike Shop character.

We offer speedy delivery across Ireland for a selected range of products as well as thorough information for all our customers including assembly guides, repair guides, size guides, willing and excellent advice and a clear and fair returns policy.

So please join us and become a vital part of our journey. All enquiries are welcome, so feel free to contact us by filling in the form below.
